Living Room Bay Window Treatments-Insiders Guide

Whether it is new material or not, homes can be decorated with simple creativity. Windows make the natural light from outside enter the home; and thus, it is one of the best parts of the home to beautify. These adornments do not always have to be expensive and difficult.

In fact, materials that are not too useful at home can be one of the frills to decorate a window. From old belts, old patterned tablecloth to fabric shower curtains and even old jewelry - these can be used as a nice collaterals to embellish a new look to home windows. DIY home decor and window coverings are becoming a fast-rising favorite among homemakers nowadays.

Here are some of the Do-It-Yourself spree to beautify household windows:

No-Sew Window Treatment

As easy as no sew, windows can have new accents and new vibes through the use of scarf, table cloth, and fabric shower curtain, as well as traditional jewelry. By simply putting up a color and decorating present curtains with some ornaments, it can give an elusive look to the window. One can also place printed stickers made for glass and made especially for windows. This type of window treatment can be easily bought from any home depot and can also help in blocking light from entering our homes. Plastic gems and trinkets can also be glued into windows to add a different texture and look into them.

Gold Chain Tieback

For plain and printed curtains at home, a gold chain tieback can be used to open up the window a bit. In accordance with style and extravagance, gold chain can add visual appeal to the windows. Gold chains can be practically bought anywhere, but you can also make your own version minus the extra cash by simple dipping ordinary rope with gold paint. Seal it with a paint sealer and you'll have something as pretty as that of the ones bought in stores.

Stenciled Draperies

Unleash some creativity and use some paint. Stenciled draperies can be customized to whatever the home motif is. Solid print patterns, no matter how big or small, can transform a plain curtain to its new attractive form. You can even spend a weekend with the children to do the stencilling as a group. It would surely be a fun and memorable experience for the whole family. You can even stencil designs in your plantation shutters to define and personalize its looks.

Leather Belt Tieback

If gold chains are not present and leather belts are starting to worn out as a daily outfit use, they can be used to fasten the window curtains. Turn those curtains to some fashion statement and still make use of old-fashioned belts at home. You can add rhinestones or paint the belts in a different color to upgrade its look.

Knotted Chiffon Café Curtains

Aside from the traditional whole curtains, various chiffon fabrics lying around at home can be tied side-by-side until they become curtains. This technique can give a relaxing and breezy atmosphere inside the house with just a simple usage of chiffon fabric, curtain rod, and scissors.

Decorating your homes need not be expensive. With a little ingenuity and resourcefulness, even the most unlikely materials inside your homes can become a one-of-a-kind decor and accessory for your simple abode.

One of the interesting DIY projects that one can add in their homes is plantation shutters. Costs are not always expensive but comparing plantation shutters cost with that of the DIY ones can prove a significant difference in your savings. If you prefer an instant change in the look of your home minus the hassles, ready-made plantation shutters is advised.
