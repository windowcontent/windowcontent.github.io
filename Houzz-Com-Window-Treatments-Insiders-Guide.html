Houzz Com Window Treatments-Insiders Guide

Various window themes and trends can pump up a home's ambiance and atmosphere. Windows are considered accessories for a more attractive and cozy house or apartment. With simple changes incorporated in the windows of your homes, a new experience and more calming vibe for your abode is within your reach. In accordance to the latest fashion and trends, windows are also expected to align with the changes in home design. As home design and motif change, window designs are also expected to go together with the flow.

A lot of window styles and designs have changed and some even went through constant innovations. Though designs from the past are surprisingly arising again, they gave a modern impression for living areas giving an interesting finish. As the post-modern time progresses, styles at home flourish with it as well.

Here are some of the 5 latest trends and innovations for today's windows:

1. Organic Materials

Everyone is getting the hint of taking care of the environment and pushing through with organic materials - not just in food diversions, bags, and other clothing, but also in terms of curtains and window decorations. It creates accents and provides green impression. Organic materials are also being developed to become more durable, sustaining the growing needs for these types of materials for homes.

2. Bamboo

Curtains do not only speak for cloth but also of other real innovative materials. Bamboo blinds are trending for those who have homes with a natural elements motif. Vacation houses and tropical-inspired homes are leaning into the bamboo-inspired window treatments not only to blend with the house's current theme but to provide a fresh and relaxing ambiance inside their abode.

3. Bold Prints and Colorful Curtains

To beef up the mood of the house, bold prints and colorful curtains are also part of the growing trend. A hyped look could be varied by using colors like red, mustard yellow, and orange, just to name a few. Moreover, bold prints of spiral, big solid patterns, and floral patterns can give emphasis on the window side of home. These offer a modern twist to the usual monotone designs that traditional homes have. The great thing about this trend is that various options and designs are being developed in the market, making it easier for home makers to find something that will match not only their needs but also their personalities.

4. Tassel Fringe and Extra Long Curtains

Taking its cue back to the old English era, tassel fringe and extra-long curtains setup is coming back again. This window treatment setup renders a classic vibe of old English chef-d'oeuvre. This look is also perfect for the classically designed homes. Long curtains bring an extra flair of elegance and grandeur, a very common look for the good old classically themed homes.

5. Electronic Blinds

Giving the post-modern feel at home, electronic blinds are the instant way to go. Its maintenance and setups are easy and contemporary. Offices are also utilizing the use of electronic blinds to give an avant-garde ambience to the business. Also known as automated blinds, these window treatments are also providing an extra sheet of security for our homes.

As home decor and design continues to progress and change, window treatments and trends are also expected to move parallel with the progress. More exciting designs and materials for window blinds and curtains are expected to be developed and be in the trend in the coming years.

Window blinds are changing and adapting to the needs of the modern home. New materials and exciting new features are expected to be developed in the future as the home decor industry continues to grow.
